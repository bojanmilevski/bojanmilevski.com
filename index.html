THE ZEBRA THEORY

The city I grew up in - the linden-scented city, is withering and falling apart right before my eyes, and I can't do anything to change the fact. It's eventual salvation or inevitable doom are the only outcomes: no intermediate state is possible, and I oppose the idea of one.
